60 Seconds!

It's game that Robot Gentleman Studio produced. see this : https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=60seconds

Short explanation

You hurry up! nuclear missile is coming. You have to equip many things before missile has arrived. maybe It's too short time but you can many things in 60seconds. You can take 3 people and radio and some food etc. It's amazing.

Important things

Food, water, radio map, axe, riple, Its all important and has own functions. You must think carefully. It's urgent situation!

Adventure

Someone have to seek any other helpful things outside. When you choose someone, It's very hard and asking serious choice.

Birthday

There are some event like birthday. But is it good to care someone's birthday under such situations? I don't know.

Distribution

Limited water, food, etc. You have to distribute these food and water. Let's thanks for food and water.

Diseases

Too much diseases and thirstiness and hungriness will overwhelm you and your family

Waiting

You have to wait and wait over and over again. Waiting food. Waiting water. Waiting help.

Craziness

Crazy is also dangerous status. Think about that situation, crazy will be very normal.

Comments

Enjoy this game and feel importance and strength of 60seconds.
